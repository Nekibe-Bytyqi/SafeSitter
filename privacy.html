At SafeSitter, we respect and protect your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose personal information that we obtain from users of our website and platform.

We do not share your personal information with third parties except in the following circumstances:

6. Your Choices

You have the following rights concerning your personal information:

  • You can update or delete your account information at any time.
  • You can opt-out of receiving promotional emails from us by following the instructions in the email.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and we will notify you via email if significant changes are made. Please review this Privacy Policy regularly for updates.
